About Us

Innocence Found was established with a singular purpose: to fight for justice and freedom for those who have been wrongfully convicted. We believe that every person deserves a fair trial and that the truth should always prevail.

Our team consists of dedicated professionals including attorneys, investigators, forensic experts, and volunteers who work together to review cases, gather evidence, and advocate for those who have been unjustly imprisoned.

Through our efforts, we have helped bring attention to numerous cases and have contributed to the exoneration of individuals who were wrongfully convicted. We continue to work tirelessly to ensure that justice is served and that innocent people are freed from wrongful imprisonment.
